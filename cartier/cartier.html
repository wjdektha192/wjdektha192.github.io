The About

About Pasha de Cartier​


With its distinctive codes and extraordinary design, the Pasha de Cartier collection is for those who dare to pave new creative paths. The new designs are true to the original 1980s models but with a more refined silhouette, distinguished by a unique chained crown that epitomizes its extroverted spirit. Our innovative QuickSwitch system brings an element of modernity to this timepiece; customize between the steel, gold and leather straps for a look that is all your own. Also equipped with a sapphire crystal case back to reveal its movement – the 1847 MC automatic caliber – this collection is a perfect hybrid of elegance and performance, created for those with bold ambitions.
